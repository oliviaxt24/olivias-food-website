Resturants


  • Sol Food

    • Sol food offers Puerto Rican cuisine Sol Food, located in San Rafael, is recognized for its Puerto Rican cuisine and vibrent atmosphere. My favorite dish is the pollo al horno (baked chicken) with either maduros (sweet plantains) or tostones (fried plantains).





  • Palette Tea House

    • Palette Tea House is known for its modern take on Cantonese food. My personal favorite dishes are the xiao long bao and the truffle duck fried rice.





  • Proposition Chicken

    • In my opinion, Proposition Chicken is one of the best places to get chicken. My favorite, the fried chicken, can be enjoyed in many ways, such as in a sandwitch, in a bowl, or by itself.
