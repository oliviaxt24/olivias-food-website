Other


  • Easy Breezy

    • Easy Breezy is one of my favorite dessert locations. They have a large variety of flavors, that offten change, and many different types of toppings. I often get vanilla bean with one of the seasonal flavors and gummy worms.





  • Yifang Taiwan Fruit Tea

    • I love Yifang Taiwan Fruit Tea. They have a huge menu with all types of drinks, and my favorite is the strawberry sago.





  • Palmetto Superfoods

    • Palmetto Superfoods has the best acai bowls. In addition to the acai base, they have so many other opptions, such as blue chia pudding and mango. I normaly get a bowl with mango and acai as the base and granola, honey, bananas, strawberries, and blueberries as the toppings.
