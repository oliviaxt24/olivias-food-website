Bakeries


  • Schuberts's Bakery

    • Schubert’s Bakery has a delicious variety of cakes, pastries, and treats. My favorites are the strawberry shortcake and the mango mousse cake.





  • Cherry Blossom Bakery

    • Located on Clement St., Cherry Blossom Bakery has some of the best pastries and sweets. My favorite treats are the strawberry shortcake and the mini pineapple custard buns.





  • 85°C Bakery

    • 85°C Bakery has so many tasty treats, from drinks to cakes to buns. They always have freshly baked breads, over 60 different types, that are inspired by European, Asian and Danish cuisines.
